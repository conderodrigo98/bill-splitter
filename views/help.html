Help

Getting started:

To start splitting your bills you have to first create a session. To do this go to create and fill the form. The page will respond with a pin, save this somewhere safe. By creating a session you allow all your friends to add what they paid from their own device.

Adding payments:

To add a payment go to edit. Use your pin (the one you saved somewhere safe!) to access your session and add your payment.
Note: the cost should be a numeric value. Inputs like $15.05 or 3.95 dollars won't get processed by the app.

Checking your session's status:

Whether you are ready to split your bills or you just want to know your payments so far, you can go to split. By splitting you get your session's whole history (payments by each person with date, description and cost) and who owns who.
